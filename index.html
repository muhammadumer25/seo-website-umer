About Karachi Nihari House

Established in 1985, Karachi Nihari House has been serving the best nihari in Karachi for over three decades. Our founder, Mr. Ahmed Khan, started with a small stall in Burns Road with a mission to preserve and share the authentic flavors of Pakistani cuisine.

Today, we continue that tradition with the same dedication to quality and taste. Our nihari is slow-cooked for hours with a secret blend of spices that has been passed down through generations, creating a rich, flavorful dish that keeps our customers coming back for more.

Whether you're craving our signature beef nihari, spicy chicken nihari, or our special mutton nihari, each dish is prepared with care and served with fresh naan and traditional garnishes.
